Step 2

Questions and answers

Responses:
1. Where did you spend your best vacations?
I spent my best vacation in Cancún. The beaches were stunning, the water was crystal clear, and I really enjoyed relaxing under the sun. It was an amazing mix of fun and relaxation.
2. When did you go there?
I went there four years ago during the summer. It was the perfect time to enjoy the beach and the warm weather.
3. How did you get there? Transport.
I got there by plane. The flight was smooth and only took a few hours, and from the airport, I took a shuttle to the hotel.
4. Who did you go with?
I went with my family. We had a great time together exploring the beaches, trying local food, and doing fun activities like snorkeling.
5. What places did you visit?
Visit different beaches, Playa del Carmen, Isla Mujeres, ruins and a cenote and the hotel area.
6. What did you do there?
Enjoying my vacation, I visited different beaches I also met people and above all I rested too much
7. Why did you go there?
I always wanted to know the sea and I really like to get to know different cultures and what better opportunity than going to Cancun.
